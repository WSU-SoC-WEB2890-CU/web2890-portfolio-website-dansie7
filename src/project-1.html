NO1

Photarity

UX Case Study

BRIEF

Project for User Experience course developing a website from start to finish built on user experience principles.

The idea behind Photarity was a photography company that donated a portion of profits to eco-based charities of the client's choice.

PERSONA

Sami Jones

Creative Visonary

A 28 year old marketing manager at a local product design firm. Seeking a photography company like Photarity to assist with sustainable marketing campaigns & promotional materials that also support a charitable cause.

"Making a
difference
in the world
starts with me."

STYLE

#EDEBEB

#69501E

#D8D5CC

#0F0706

PROTOTYPE

USABILITY TESTING

Conducted usability test with 5 participants, 4 female & 1 male. The target age ranged from 27 to 36 years of aged. All reported spending substantial amounts of time online throughout the day implying that they have at the very least basic knowledge of how to navigate pages online.

TASKS

Each participant was asked to complete 3 step scenarios under the pretense of being a brand consultant for a water bottle company.

  1. Locate images with mountain backdrops.
  2. Find out information about the photographer.
  3. Locate where to contact the photographer.

FINDINGS

ISSUE

Users clicked on the landing page image expecting to be directed to the Gallery page.

SOLUTION

Users clicked on the landing page image expecting to be directed to the Gallery page.

ISSUE

Users clicked on individual image in gallery expecting to see info about the photographer.

SOLUTION

Add content on gallery page to direct users to meet the photographer.
 

ISSUE

Users expected price & size to be listed with each image.
 

SOLUTION

Add dropdown with available sizes & prices.
